Starvail

Presence the birth of a new star.

In a world were humanity has ascended, only contaminated wastes populated by androids remain. To erase one and for all the malware known as "Existential Dread", these androids must climb Starvail Tower. Take control of one of them, be witness to the birth of a new star and find meaning to your immortality!

  • Category: SciFi Shooter Sidescroller
